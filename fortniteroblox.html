How To Play Fortnite And Roblox

Create an account with your school email on github.com

Click the big button on https://github.com/3kh0/DesktopOnCodespaces

Create the fork

Go to https://github.com/features/codespaces

Click create new codespace

Select the thing you just created in the "select repository" button

Then click create codespace

Open console inside the codespace

Paste this in the terminal and enter:

curl -O https://git.mollomm1.dev/Mollomm1/DesktopOnCodespaces/raw/branch/main/install.sh chmod +x install.sh

Wait for it to load and go to ports

Click on the link

Click on the lion

Install the lion

Go to geforce/now.gg inside the lion

Open Fortnite/Roblox
